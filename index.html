Proof it's not mystical

Try one rep right now.

  1. Inhale through your nose for a count of 4.
  2. Exhale slow through pursed lips for a count of 8.
  3. Repeat five times. About 90 seconds.

The count matters less than the long exhale: that's the switch that downshifts your nervous system. Now imagine having that switch drilled deep enough to work at 2 a.m. on a barricade call.
